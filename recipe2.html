Spicy Grilled Chicken

A smoky, fiery grilled chicken breast marinated in paprika, cayenne, and lime. Perfect for a healthy, high-protein meal that packs a punch.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 12 mins
Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts (boneless)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Lime (juiced)
  • 2 cloves Garlic (crushed)
  • Salt & Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare Marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate: Place the chicken in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Ensure both sides are coated. Let it sit for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Heat the Grill: Preheat your grill pan or outdoor grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates.
  4. Grill: Place chicken on the grill. Cook for about 6 minutes per side. The chicken is done when the internal temperature reaches 75°C.
  5. Rest: Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. This keeps the juices inside!
