Classic Chicken Burger

A juicy, flame-grilled beef patty topped with melted cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce, and ripe tomatoes on a toasted brioche bun. The ultimate comfort food.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 8 mins
Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken Patty
  • 1 Brioche Bun
  • 1 slice Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce Leaf & Tomato slices
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Butter (for toasting)
  • Salt & Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Season the Patty: Generously season both sides of the chicken patty with salt and black pepper. Use your thumb to make a small indent in the center to prevent bulging.
