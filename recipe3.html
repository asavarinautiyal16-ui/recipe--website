Chocolate Lava Cake

A decadent, warm chocolate cake with a gooey, molten center. It requires precise timing to get that perfect "lava" flow, making it a true baker's challenge.

Prep: 15 mins
Bake: 12 mins
Level: Hard

Ingredients

  • 100g High-quality Dark Chocolate
  • 50g Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Granulated Sugar
  • 1 tbsp All-purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Cocoa Powder (for dusting)
  • A pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven to 200°C. Grease two ramekins with butter and dust them lightly with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt Chocolate: Gently melt the chocolate and butter together using a double boiler or in short bursts in the microwave. Stir until smooth.
  3. Whisk Eggs: In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar together until the mixture is thick and pale (about 2-3 minutes).
  4. Combine: Fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture. Sift in the flour and salt, then gently fold until just combined.
  5. Bake: Divide the batter between the ramekins. Bake for exactly 12 minutes. The sides should be firm, but the center should still look soft.
  6. Serve: Let them cool for 1 minute, then carefully invert onto a plate. Serve immediately with vanilla ice cream!
