Creamy Garlic Pasta

A velvety, restaurant-style pasta made with fresh garlic, heavy cream, and parmesan cheese. Perfect for a quick 20-minute dinner.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti or Fettuccine
  • 4 cloves Garlic, finely minced
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp Salted Butter
  • Fresh Parsley for garnish
  • Salt & Black Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Sauté Garlic: While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant (don't let it brown!).
  3. Make the Sauce: Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Let it thicken slightly for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Combine: Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted. Add the cooked pasta directly into the skillet.
  5. Finish: Toss well, adding a splash of pasta water if the sauce is too thick. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
