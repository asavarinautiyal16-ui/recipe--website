Classic Margherita Pizza

The quintessential Neapolitan pizza. Topped with simple, fresh ingredients: San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and extra-virgin olive oil.

Prep: 20 mins
Bake: 10 mins
Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 250g Pizza Dough
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Puree
  • 150g Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt & Dried Oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to its highest setting (usually 250°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it inside.
  2. Shape the Dough: On a floured surface, stretch the dough into a 10-12 inch circle. Keep the edges slightly thicker for a crust.
  3. Sauce it up: Spread a thin layer of tomato puree over the dough, leaving an inch of space around the edges. Sprinkle a pinch of salt.
  4. Add Cheese: Tear the fresh mozzarella into chunks and distribute evenly over the sauce.
  5. Bake: Place in the oven for 8-10 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Finish: Remove from oven. Immediately top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
